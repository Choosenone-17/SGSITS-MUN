About SGSITS-MUN

SGSITS-MUN is the official Model United Nations club of Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science (SGSITS), Indore. Established with the vision to foster global awareness, diplomacy, and leadership skills among students, SGSITS-MUN has been actively organizing conferences, workshops, and training sessions since its inception.

SGSITS-MUN is the official Model United Nations conference of Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science (SGSITS), Indore. Established with the vision to foster global awareness, diplomacy, and leadership among students, SGSITS-MUN has been a platform for meaningful debate and dialogue on pressing international issues. Through meticulously organized committees, engaging debates, and immersive simulations of UN proceedings, SGSITS-MUN provides participants with the opportunity to develop critical thinking, public speaking, and negotiation skills. Our conference not only encourages deeper understanding of global politics but also inspires delegates to step into the shoes of world leaders and changemakers. At SGSITS-MUN, we celebrate diversity, collaboration, and the exchange of ideas. By bringing together passionate students from across the region and beyond, we aim to cultivate future leaders who are prepared to navigate the complexities of an interconnected world. Join us in shaping conversations, building perspectives, and creating impact — one debate at a time.

About Theme

The theme for SGSITS-MUN 2025, "DIGIT : DEMOCRACY,INNOVATION,GLOBAL,INCLUSION,TECHNOLOGY" Digit is our vision where democratic values,innovative ideas,inclusive growth,welfare,and technological progress come together to shape a fairer,progressive,and truly connected world.
