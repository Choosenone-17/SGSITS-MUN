LOK SABHA

Committee Description

The Lok Sabha, or House of the People, is the lower house of India's bicameral Parliament, playing a crucial role in the country's legislative process. Comprising directly elected representatives from various constituencies across India, the Lok Sabha is responsible for making laws, debating national issues, and overseeing the executive branch of government. It holds significant powers, including the ability to pass budgets and scrutinize government policies. The Lok Sabha operates under a parliamentary system, where the majority party or coalition forms the government, and its members represent the diverse interests of India's vast population.
