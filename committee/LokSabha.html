Namaste Delegates,

It is my honour and greatest pleasure to serve as the Chairperson of the Lok Sabha Committee in the first-ever iteration of the SGSITS MUN-2025.

I still remember filling up my first ever delegate form back in 7th grade and have been enthusiastic about MUNs ever since.

Honestly, I am a pretty compassionate and humble person, someone you can always count on for any kind of support. I am also a huge movie enthusiast which means I will probably spend my weekend staring at my laptop screen, watching the same Bollywood movie for the 10th time.

I am a person who loves exploring new things. Finding logic and reasoning behind everything is what my mind always looks for. I am a person who loves Physics with all her heart and can discuss black holes, galaxies, and rockets for hours. Geopolitics has always been something close to me, and my day is never complete without watching or reading the same. Regards,
Paridhi Arora

LOK SABHA

Committee Description

The Lok Sabha, or House of the People, is the lower house of India's bicameral Parliament, playing a crucial role in the country's legislative process. Comprising directly elected representatives from various constituencies across India, the Lok Sabha is responsible for making laws, debating national issues, and overseeing the executive branch of government. It holds significant powers, including the ability to pass budgets and scrutinize government policies. The Lok Sabha operates under a parliamentary system, where the majority party or coalition forms the government, and its members represent the diverse interests of India's vast population.
